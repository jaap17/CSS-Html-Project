CAREERS

Research Chemist

Job Function
Design and develop new engineering acrylic adhesives and sealants; research, analyze and design acrylic oligomers and polymers sfor use in design of new adhesives; perform chemical, physical and environmental testing of adhesives during product design and development; prepare product submissions and perform trial productions for new products; prepare raw material specification files and material safety data sheet; provide technical analysis for production, sales and marketing; provide technical support to customers.
Job duties also require use of the following tools
Oligomer and Polymer synthesis, viscosity measurements, Tensile Testing Machine, Torque measurements, TA instruments and FTIR. Qualifications
MS (or PhD) in Chemistry with 7 years related exp. (3 years with PhD).To apply, send in your resume to sales@parsonadhesives.com
